How To Play

Hello! Welcome to Astro Blasters. In this game, the class will be set up into teams of three randomly. Each team will have 2 hearts, and their goal is to be the last one standing.

Every round, a new question appears, and a person dedicated to hitting a buzzer attempts to buzz in the fastest. The fastest team may attempt to answer the question. if they are correct, their attack meter charges by one. After two successful questions are answered in a row, the team with the full charge meter may choose which team to attack. However, if a question is answered incorrectly, the charge meter resets to zero.
